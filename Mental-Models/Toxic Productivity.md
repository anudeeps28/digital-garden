# Toxic Productivity

## Idea
Equating self-worth with output leads to burnout — productivity should serve life, not replace it.

## Definition
Toxic productivity is the mindset where being busy is treated as inherently virtuous, and rest or enjoyment is guilt-laden. It shows up as filling every moment with tasks, feeling anxious when idle, and measuring a good day by output rather than meaning. The irony: this approach is actually less productive over time, as it exhausts the physical and mental resources needed for sustained creative work. Being busy is not the goal — doing meaningful work is.
