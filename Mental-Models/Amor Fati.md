# Amor Fati

## Idea
Love your fate — embrace everything that happens, good or bad, as something to be desired.

## Definition
Amor Fati is a Stoic philosophy meaning "love of fate." Rather than wishing things were different or ruminating over past mistakes, you embrace your circumstances fully — not merely tolerating them, but actively loving them as necessary parts of your story. Ruminating over what could have been only brings exhaustion; the mindset of letting life surprise you brings freedom.

## Source
Stoic philosophy; Maya Angelou quote on courage and embracing life; personal reflection.

> "I've always had the feeling that life loves the liver of it... Courage is probably the most important of the virtues because without courage you cannot practice any of the other virtues." — Maya Angelou
