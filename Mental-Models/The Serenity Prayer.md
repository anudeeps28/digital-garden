# The Serenity Prayer

## Idea
Accept what you cannot change, change what you can, and develop the wisdom to know the difference.

## Definition
> God, grant me the serenity to accept the things I cannot change, courage to change the things I can, and wisdom to know the difference.

While framed as a prayer, this is a practical Stoic operating principle. It directly encodes [[The Dichotomy of Control]]: identify what is in your power, act on it; identify what is not, release it. The "wisdom to know the difference" is the hardest part — and the most important practice.

## Source
Attributed to Reinhold Niebuhr; adopted by Alcoholics Anonymous and widely used in therapeutic contexts.
