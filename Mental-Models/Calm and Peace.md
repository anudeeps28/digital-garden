# Calm and Peace

## Idea
Calm and peace are not found — they are cultivated through consistent practices that stabilize the mind.

## Definition
Calm and peace are not default states in a world of notifications, news, and social comparison. They must be actively built through practice. The core practices: physical exercise (releases tension from the body), meditation (trains attention and equanimity), and journaling (externalizes and processes thoughts). These three create the conditions for [[Mental Clarity and Meaning]] — when the mind is quiet, you can hear what actually matters.

## Source
Personal practice; related to Stoic philosophy, mindfulness traditions.
