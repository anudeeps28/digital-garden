# Happiness

## Idea
Happiness is a practice built from within — not a reward that external circumstances deliver.

## Definition
Happiness is not a destination reached by acquiring things or achieving goals. Due to [[Hedonic Adaptation]], even major positive life changes return to baseline quickly. Durable wellbeing comes from meaning (connection between actions and values), engagement (flow states, craft, learning), relationships, and contribution — not from possessions or status. The practice of happiness involves protecting [[Calm and Peace]], reducing unnecessary suffering, and doing work that is intrinsically rewarding.

## Source
Positive psychology research; Stoic philosophy; personal synthesis.
