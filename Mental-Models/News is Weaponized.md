# News is Weaponized

## Idea
Media is designed by experts to hijack your attention and emotions — consuming it uncritically destroys your mental clarity.

## Definition
We live in an era of information overload, but it isn't neutral. Social statisticians, psychologists, and UX researchers are paid to figure out how to addict you to news and social media. What gets shown is whatever generates the most engagement — which means outrage, fear, and controversy. You are letting someone else decide what to be angry about, and you can't do anything about most of what they show you anyway. The most effective response: focus on your own work, your own life, and the people who actually matter to you.
