# Consistency

## Idea
Consistent small effort over time is the single most important factor in achieving anything worthwhile.

## Definition
No skill, book, habit, or opportunity matters without consistency. It is the meta-skill that multiplies everything else. Showing up every day — even imperfectly, even reluctantly — compounds in ways that feel invisible in the short term and become undeniable in the long term. The guitar player who does daily chord changes, the runner who doesn't break the chain — their progress seems slow day-to-day but transforms over months. The key insight is often not motivation or talent but the act of *getting started* repeatedly (see [[The Flywheel Concept]]).

## Source
Personal experience with guitar practice and running; James Clear's work on habits.
