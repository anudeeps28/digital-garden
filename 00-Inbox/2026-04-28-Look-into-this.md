Managing Ambiguity Checklist

1\.    Resolve uncertainties immediately by asking questions or proposing solutions

2\.    Clearly state your plan, what you’re working on now, and why it matters

3\.    Prioritize tasks based on stakeholder needs and deadlines

4\.    Seek feedback regularly to adjust your course 

Critical-Issue Resolution Checklist

1\.    Capture all actionable tasks from internal or client meetings

2\.    Schedule a 15-minute alignment meeting with your manager

3\.    Define the next concrete steps and dedicate 1 hour to execute them

4\.    Request rapid feedback and pivot if needed

5\.    Tackle follow-up work under a tight 30–60 minute window6.    

Repeat steps 4–5, involving teammates if additional support is required

7\.    Finalize deliverables and update documentation

8\.    Walk stakeholders through your PPT/sheet and formally close out the issue
